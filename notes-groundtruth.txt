## Spend Display Discrepancy – Requirements Notes

Customers are reporting that the spend amount displayed in the app does not always match the total shown in their transaction list.

Product wants something visible to customers quickly so they know the issue is being investigated and can trust the app again.

Engineering is still investigating the root cause. Possible causes include delayed transaction updates, caching, pending transactions, refunds, or an issue with the spend calculation service.

A temporary customer-facing banner should be added while the investigation continues.

The banner should:

* Inform customers that some spend totals may take time to update
* Confirm that the issue is being investigated
* Avoid saying that the issue has been fixed
* Avoid directing customers to the transaction list unless Engineering confirms it is accurate
* Be approved by Compliance before release

Engineering must investigate and confirm:

* Which customers are affected
* Which transaction types are affected
* Whether pending transactions, refunds, or completed transactions are causing the discrepancy
* How long incorrect amounts remain visible
* Whether the transaction list displays the correct amount
* The root cause and permanent fix

Customer Operations will provide complaint examples and complaint volumes.

The Android login crash raised during the meeting is not part of this requirement and should be tracked separately.

The temporary banner is expected within approximately two to three days, subject to Engineering findings and Compliance approval.

It has not yet been decided whether the spend total should remain visible, be temporarily hidden, or display an additional warning beside it.
